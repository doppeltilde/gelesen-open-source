## What's changed?
- On Apple devices you can now use ”Game Center” as an option to sign in.
- Images are now shown inline with the conversation history, making it easier to see them again.
- Fixed an issue where the chat avatar and name were not center aligned at the top.
- Fixed an issue where AV1 encoded videos would not play any visuals.
